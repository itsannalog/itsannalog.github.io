Annabel Lee

A short puzzle experience based on the poem by Edgar Allan Poe, created for Game Design 1. Make your way to the cemetery and discover what truly happened to Annabel Lee.




About Development:

Created with Unity, this is my first game using the engine. All assets, including sound, were created within a 3 week period for my solo final project.
