Stevens Esports

Stevens Esports is the campus esports organization at Stevens Institute of Technology, hosting collegiate esports teams for multiple titles including Overwatch, League of Legends, Hearthstone, and more. I founded Stevens Esports in late 2018, and have been very involved in the development process. Graphic work I've done for Stevens Esports includes the logo, a Twitter header, welcome week graphics, match scorecards, Twitch graphics, merch design, and other miscellaneous items.
