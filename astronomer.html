Untitled Astronomer Game

Untitled Astronomer Game is a short visual novel game where you influence astronomers of past to make different decisions and ultimately rewrite history. You are a space traveler who accidentally got teleported into the lives of famous astronomers, choose between Copernicus and Galileo. Snoop around and befriend them without causing suspicion or forever altering the course of history! If you fail, you cease to exist. Sorry, but we need to know about astronomy for space travel.


Created for HST320 as part of a group final project.




About Development:

Created with ren.py, this is a short game drawing inspiration from multiple other visual novel games, and title inspiration from Untitled Goose Game.


For this project, I contributed the original pitch, all art assets, programming of several of Galileo's scenes, and the final presentation.
