Featured Game:

FORCE 2
November 2018 - SGDC Game Jam D 3rd Place

FORCE 2: FORCE Remastered is a 2-4 player arena-style fighting game in which you can only kill your opponents via the environment. To play, you will need at least one friend, at least two Xbox controllers, and a Windows PC. Each character has unique abilities, and there are many ways to die, including by your own hand. Play some rounds in the arena- the first player to 5 or 9 points will win the match. Created with help from Adam Gincel and Lachlan Wright.
