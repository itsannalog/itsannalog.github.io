cope

cope is a small technical demo for a platmormer mechanic I thought would be interesting. Elements turn on and off and change the landscape of the level, presenting common platforming challenges (timed jumps, spiky obstacles) but with a creative twist uncommon to most platformers.


About the Game:

cope is a single player platformer featuring world duality. It requires a Windows machine and a keyboard to play. Press space to jump, arrow keys to move left and right, and shift to see secret platforms as you navigate your brain through scary and dangerous places and back into your skull.
